## Scrum Events

### Sprint Planning
The team conducts a Sprint Planning meeting at the beginning of each sprint to define the sprint goal and select items from the product backlog for the sprint backlog.

### Daily Scrum
The team holds a Daily Scrum every day for 15 minutes to inspect progress and plan the next 24 hours.

### Sprint Review
At the end of the sprint, the team conducts a Sprint Review to demonstrate completed work and gather stakeholder feedback.

### Sprint Retrospective
After the Sprint Review, the team reflects on the sprint to identify improvements for future sprints.

## Scrum Artifacts

### Product Backlog
The Product Backlog is an ordered list of everything needed for the product and is managed by the Product Owner.

### Sprint Backlog
The Sprint Backlog consists of selected Product Backlog items and a plan for delivering them during the sprint.

### Increment
The Increment is the sum of all completed Product Backlog items and must meet the Definition of Done.
